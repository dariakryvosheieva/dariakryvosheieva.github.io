I'm an MIT sophomore from Ukraine studying AI and Linguistics.

My research interests are:

  • making high-quality language technologies available to everyone, including indigenous populations and speakers of digitally underresourced languages;
  • advancing computer systems' language comprehension and reasoning abilities.

Currently, I'm working as an undergraduate researcher at CPL, where I'm investigating how multilingual LLMs make decisions related to grammar.

In high school, I represented Ukraine at the International Linguistics Olympiad, which taught me to think outside the box and look for imaginative problem solutions. I still support the linguistics olympiad community by creating and reviewing problems.
