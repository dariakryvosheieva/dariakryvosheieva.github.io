Welcome!

I'm an MIT junior from Ukraine studying AI and Linguistics.

Background

Fulcrum ⋅ In January 2026, I participated in a one-month research fellowship at Fulcrum, working on predicting coding agent performance on new tasks and benchmarks.

Jina AI ⋅ In Summer 2025, I interned at Jina AI, building a code embedding model. I learned about information retrieval, neural search, and industry-scale model training.

Syntax Neurons ⋅ In 2025, I worked with Greta Tuckute, Andrea de Varda, and Prof. Evelina Fedorenko at EvLab on localizing neural units implicated in syntax processing in LLMs. We discovered that different types of syntactic agreement (e.g., subject-verb and determiner-noun agreement) consistently engage the same units, even across languages. See the preprint!

Targeted Syntactic Evaluations ⋅ In 2024, I worked with Prof. Roger Levy at the Computational Psycholinguistics Lab on applying the targeted syntactic evaluation methodology to assess multilingual LMs' knowledge of grammars of low-resource languages. I presented the paper that resulted from this research at the LoResLM workshop at COLING 2025 in Abu Dhabi, UAE.
