I'm a rising sophomore at MIT from Ukraine pursuing a double major in AI and Linguistics.

My interests include NLP, computer vision, and mechanistic interpretability. Currently, I'm working as an undergraduate researcher at the MIT Computational Psycholinguistics Lab where I'm investigating how multilingual LLMs make decisions related to grammar.

In high school, I represented Ukraine at the International Linguistics Olympiad, which taught me to think outside the box of my native language and culture and to look for imaginative problem solutions. I still support the linguistics olympiad community by authoring and testing problems.
