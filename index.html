Background

Low-Resource NLP

I'm passionate about making language technologies more linguistically diverse and inclusive.

Targeted Syntactic Evaluations ⋅ In 2024, I worked with Prof. Roger Levy at the Computational Psycholinguistics Lab on applying the targeted syntactic evaluation methodology to assess multilingual LMs' knowledge of grammars of low-resource languages. The paper is currently under review at LoResLM @ COLING 2025.

Phonetic Transfer Learning ⋅ My team's final project for MIT's NLP class explores finetuning on phonetic input as a means to facilitate knowledge transfer between related languages in multilingual LMs.

OCR ⋅ I trained a suite of OCR engines for low-resource writing systems and integrated them with a usable Flask app. See the GitHub repo for the app, model training code, and a technical report.

Linguistics Olympiads

I represented Ukraine at four editions of the International Linguistics Olympiad:

  • 2023 (Bansko, Bulgaria)
  • 2022 (Castletown, Isle of Man)
  • 2021 (remote, hosted by Ventspils, Latvia)
  • 2019 (Yongin, South Korea)
The participation experience taught me to think outside the box of my native language and culture and look for imaginative problem solutions.
